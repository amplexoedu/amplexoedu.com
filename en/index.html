✦ Our Teacher

Teacher Clarissa Vergara

Mensa Member Intertel Member 14+ Years Exp.

With over 14 years of experience in education, Clarissa is pleased to share her passion for learning with all those seeking to improve their knowledge and skills.

Her goal is to provide effective tools to optimize the learning process of students. She also works with pedagogical guidance of high-performance students, helping them reach their maximum potential.

Clarissa is a member of Intertel and Mensa, the largest, oldest and most famous high IQ society in the world. She has extensive experience in Olympiad training, having served as a Mathematics teacher for the OBM.

Her background includes a degree in Mathematics, a postgraduate degree in Neuropsychology, and complementary studies in Neuropedagogy, High Abilities/Giftedness, and School Management.

About the Amplexo Club

📚 Curricular Enrichment

Our learning environments focus on Mathematics, Science, Astronomy, strategic games and technology. Our goal is to offer the necessary challenges so children feel confident to participate in competitions or study autonomously content the regular school doesn't offer.

🏅 Olympic Journey

Our Olympiad sector focuses on preparing but also supporting students on this journey. We have materials targeted at the main national and international olympiads, study notebooks, classes and constant meetings to explain how olympiads work.

At Amplexo Club, we value all achievements. Regardless of medals or trophies, every step matters.

🏫 How It Works

  • Live classes, Q&A sessions and recorded platform
  • Participation in Olympic Competitions
  • Classes from basic to advanced
  • Events and extra activities
  • Family support (WhatsApp Group)
