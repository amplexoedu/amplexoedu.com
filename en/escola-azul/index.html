What is the Blue School Program?

A national initiative that integrates the ocean into school curricula and encourages projects that mobilize communities to protect marine life.

🌊 Ocean literacy and interdisciplinary learning

The importance of oceans for life on Earth gains a creative approach at Clube Amplexo. Our students go beyond traditional content and transform knowledge into original productions.

The theme connects Biology, Geography, Arts and Citizenship. Even far from the sea, students understand how the ocean directly impacts climate, food systems, the economy and society's future.

🎯 Student leadership in practice

In Blue School, students act as agents of change. They research, create and share knowledge through different media to expand environmental awareness.

  • Original student productions focused on social impact
  • Audiovisual language for science communication
  • Connection between academic excellence and environmental responsibility
  • Engagement of families and community
